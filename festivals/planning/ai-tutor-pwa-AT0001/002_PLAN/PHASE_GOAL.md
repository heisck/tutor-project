# Phase Goal: 002_PLAN

**Phase:** 002_PLAN | **Status:** Pending | **Type:** Planning

## Phase Objective

Convert ingested content into lesson-ready structures including concepts, dependencies, teaching order, and difficulty analysis.

## Context

Before teaching can happen, the system must understand the material deeply and organize it into a structured learning flow.

## Focus

- Concept extraction
- Dependency mapping
- Difficulty detection
- Lesson structuring
- Quiz injection planning

## Key Question

How can raw content be transformed into structured teaching units without skipping important concepts?

## Outputs

- Lesson map
- Concept graph
- Difficulty tags
- Teaching flow structure

## Success Criteria

- Concepts identified
- Dependencies mapped
- Difficult areas detected
- Output usable for tutor engine

## Constraints

- No concept skipping
- Must preserve traceability
- No UI or teaching output yet
